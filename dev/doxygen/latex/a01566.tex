\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
\subsubsection*{template$<$typename ST$>$\newline
struct Ikarus\+::\+Materials\+::\+Neo\+Hooke\+T$<$ ST $>$}
\[ \psi(\BC) = \frac{\mu}{2} (\tr \BC-3- 2 \log \sqrt{\det \BC}) + \frac{\la}{2} (\log \sqrt{\det \BC})^2 ,\] where $ \BC $ denotes the right Cauchy-\/\+Green strain tensor.

The second Piola-\/\+Kirchhoff stresses are computed as \[ \BS(\BC) =\fracpt{\psi(\BC)}{\BC} = \mu (\BI-\BC^{-1}) + \la \log \sqrt{\det \BC}  \BC^{-1},\]

and the material tangent moduli are computed as \[ \BBC(\BC) =\fracpt{^2\psi(\BC)}{\BC^2} =  \la \BC^{-1} \otimes  \BC^{-1} + 2 (\mu- \la \log
\sqrt{\det \BC} ) \CI,\] where $ \CI_{IJKL} =  \frac{1}{2}({(\BC^{-1})}^{IK}{(\BC^{-1})}^{JL}+{(\BC^{-1})}^{IL} {(\BC^{-1})}^{JK}).$
