\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
\subsubsection*{template$<$typename ST$>$\newline
struct Ikarus\+::\+Materials\+::\+St\+Venant\+Kirchhoff\+T$<$ ST $>$}
\[ \psi(\BE) = \frac{\la}{2} (\tr \BE)^2   +\mu \tr (\BE^2) ,\] where $ \BE $ denotes the Green-\/\+Lagrangian strain.

The second Piola-\/\+Kirchhoff stresses are computed as \[ \BS(\BE) =\fracpt{\psi(\BE)}{\BE} = \la \tr \BE \BI  +2 \mu \BE,\]

and the material tangent moduli are computed as \[ \BBC(\BE) =\fracpt{^2\psi(\BE)}{\BE^2} =  \la \tr \BE \CI  +2 \mu \CI^{\mathrm{sym}},\] where $ \CI_{IJKL} =  \de_{IJ}\de_{KL}$ and $ \CI_{IJKL}^\mathrm{sym} =  \frac{1}{2}(\de_{IK}\de_{JL}+
\de_{IL}\de_{JK})$.
