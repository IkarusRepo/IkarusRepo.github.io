\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
\subsubsection*{template$<$typename ST\+\_\+$>$\newline
struct Ikarus\+::\+Materials\+::\+Gent\+T$<$ ST\+\_\+ $>$}
The energy is computed as \[ \hat{\Psi}(\la_1, \la_2, \la_3) = -\frac{\mu}{2} J_m \ln{(1-\frac{W_1 - 3}{J_m})}, \] with $ J_m > W_1-3$.

The first derivatives w.\+r.\+t the total principal stretches are \[ \fracpt{\Psi}{\la_i} = \frac{\mu J_m}{2(J_m - W_1) + 6}\fracpt{W_1}{\la_i}. \]

The second derivatives w.\+r.\+t the total principal stretches are \[ \fracpt{^2 \Psi}{\la_i\partial\la_j} =
\frac{\mu}{2\al} (\fracpt{^2 W_1}{\la_i\partial\la_j} +
\frac{1}{\al J_m} \fracpt{W_1}{\la_i} \fracpt{W_1}{\la_j}) -
\delta_{ij} \frac{\mu}{2\al\la_i} \fracpt{W_1}{\la_i}, \] with $ \al = 1 - \frac{W_1 - 3}{J_m} $.

\begin{DoxyRemark}{Remarks}
See \cite{bergstromMechanicsSolidPolymers2015} for details on this material. For information on the deviatoric invariant $ W_1 $, see \mbox{\hyperlink{a01538}{Deviatoric\+Invariants}}.
\end{DoxyRemark}
