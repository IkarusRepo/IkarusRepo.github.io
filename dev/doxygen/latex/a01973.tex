\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}

\begin{DoxyTemplParams}{Template Parameters}
{\em RT} & A template type with parameters (typename, int, int). The first parameter is the data type, and the next two parameters are the grid dimension and the world dimension. It checks for various instantiations to ensure they meet the Result\+Type concept. Specifically, it checks for the nested types \textquotesingle{}type\textquotesingle{}, \textquotesingle{}Vectorizer\textquotesingle{}, \textquotesingle{}Matricizer\textquotesingle{}, and the presence of a to\+String function returning a std\+::string. \\
